About

  • Location

    Proudly headquartered in beautiful Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

  • Mission

    Our goal is to share our passion for specialty coffee. We aim to make freshly roasted coffee accessible and affordable to the masses and only roast to order to ensure maximum freshness.

  • Equipment

    We roast on a Quest M3 home roaster in small 150g batches. Each roast is precisely monitored using Artisan software and thermocouples to monitor roast development of each batch.
